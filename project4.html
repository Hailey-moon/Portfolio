Spark! Graphic Design Test Project

This is my submission to the application for Boston University's Spark! Graphic Design Internship. The prompt was the create an eye-catching promotional graphic for Demo Day which students can share with their communities and invite to this event. With unspecified colors or fonts, the test simply required to use my own knowledge of Spark! to mimic the previous style Spark! has displayed and add in my own touch.
